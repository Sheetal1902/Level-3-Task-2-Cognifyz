Web Developer Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

Join us for an exciting internship program where you can gain real-world experience and mentorship from industry professionals. Our program lasts for 6 months, during which you will work on impactful projects and grow your skills.

Highlights

  • Real-world experience on live projects
  • One-on-one mentorship with experienced developers
  • Opportunities to work with modern technologies
  • Collaborative and supportive team environment

Qualifications

We are looking for candidates with the following skills:

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Basic understanding of version control systems (e.g., Git)
  • Familiarity with frontend frameworks like React or Angular
  • Problem-solving skills and a passion for learning
